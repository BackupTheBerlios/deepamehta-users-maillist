DeepaMehta 4.0.11 will address the incompleteness of its data model implementation, in particular "Cardinality Many".
https://trac.deepamehta.de/ticket/76
Also the GUI will be extended in this regard. E.g. the Person form is about to show a "Add another Email Address" button. At this point, the need to make the nested topic structure visible also in the content panel becomes more urgent. This will be addressed in 4.0.11 as well.
https://trac.deepamehta.de/ticket/104

Originally, the topic scheduled for 4.0.11 was "Access Control". But it appeared "Cardinality Many" is a requirement for that: a Access Control List contains *many* ACL Entries.

DeepaMehta 4.0.11 is scheduled for the end of April.
Access Control will follow in 4.0.12 roughly at the end of May.
