> In the wiki you may find instructions for installing this application
> into your local DeepaMehta installation. With this Add on, you can
> export so called "DefaultViews" out of DeepaMehta which than can be
> imported by the Flash piece of software. This is entirely written in
> ActionScript 3, as you said not really BETA yet and is just made to
> consume not to edit different media types.
>
> http://deepamehta.newthinking.net/wiki/en/Add_on:_Info_Touch
>
> Currently there is no patch for installing it, so you may have to
> read/write the topicid's, logged while clicking onto your resp.
> TopicTypes (Video, Audio, etc. ), add those into the InfoTouch.java
> Interface and then compile the source for your installation.
>
> Just let us know if you want to get into more "deepa-talk" :)
>
> Cheers !
