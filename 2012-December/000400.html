Hi dear deepamehta users and friends,

I am happy to announce that the firefox plugin is up to date.

Here is what it helps you do:
The DeepaMehta Notetaker (a web browser plugin) let users extract
individual structured items from web pages as they browse, and store and
annotate them in a shared repository for access by others.

It is designed to bring together web surfers using the
Firefox Web Browser and the upcoming DeepaMehta 4.0.13 release.

Here are all details with installation and usage hints (English):
http://deepamehta.de/en/content/deepamehta-firefox-plugin

Hier finden Sie alle Details zur Installation und Nutzung (German):
https://www.deepamehta.de/de/content/deepamehta-firefox-plugin-10

Here is the current direct download link:
http://www.deepamehta.de/sites/default/files/plugins/notetaker-deepamehta.org-1.0_0.xpi

I am looking forward to improve on this and I am in the hope that it
works for you as expected.

Cheers,
Malte

- possibly provide also a "Search in DeepaMehta"-Input Field
