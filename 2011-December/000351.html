Dear DeepaMehta afficionado,

you can support the DeepaMehta project be voting it for the WissensWert public award before Dec 13. Its easy (despite the page is in german):
1) go to
https://docs.google.com/a/wikimedia.de/spreadsheet/viewform?hl=de&formkey=dEFkNHRNWmlGbGpJeURoZVJmVjBabmc6MQ#gid=0
2) Select DeepaMehta (project 44) and press "Senden".
You're done.

The public award would get us 2000 Euro which the DeepaMehta project could make a very good use of.

The WissensWert contest is hold by the Wikimedia Deutschland foundation and rewards ideas and projects that support free knowledge. DeepaMehta already took the first round and is now eligible for the public award. Find our jury convincing submission here:
http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/WissensWert_2011/44-DeepaMehta_–_Maintain_your_head,_and_share_it

BTW: the project is alive and kicking. DeepaMehta 4.0.6 has been released recently.
https://trac.deepamehta.de/
