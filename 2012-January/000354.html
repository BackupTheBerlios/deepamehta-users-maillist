Hi Asif,
the Generic Topic is currently called "Note"; just type it's
name into the "title" field. The search functionality makes
more sense as soon as you have added/ imported many of your
own items in addition to the existing topics that are mostly
used "under the hood" by the system. Currently, the import
function is broken, but after you type a few generic items you
may search for them in the following way: use the drop-down
lists left of "search" to set "By type" and "Note", then press
"search", then click the appearing bucket. Then on the right-hand
side -- which is the easy principle since DM 2, see
http://www.deepamehta.de/wiki/en/User_Guide#On_the_right:_Some_Details_%28Property_Panel%29
your generic topics will appear.
Good luck
Matthias
