Dear DeepaMehta friends,

On May 31, 2007 DeepaMehta was introduced at the Google Developer Day  
2007 in Hamburg/Germany. In his presentation "DeepaMehta -- the  
Metatool for the Google-Cosmos", DeepaMehta CEO Andreas Gebhard  
envisaged the integration of various Google APIs (search, mail, maps,  
...) into the DeepaMehta semantic desktop.

The Google Developer Day 2007 took place all over the world  
simultaneously and its various sessions are available as web-video.

Screenshots of DeepaMehta-Google integration scenario:
www.deepamehta.de/google-developer-day-2007/deepamehta-google- 
developer-day-2007.pdf

Homepage of Google Developer Day 2007:
http://code.google.com/events/developerday/

Cheers
Jörg R.
