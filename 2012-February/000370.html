Hi DeepaMehta subscribers,

please go with these 2 rules:

1) The deepamehta-users and deepamehta-devel lists are strictly English. Don't post in other languages.
2) Do *not* cross-posting. If you want reach the users *and* developers post to the *users* list only.
    The developers are supposed to subscribe the users list too.

We want the subscribers to feel good.
