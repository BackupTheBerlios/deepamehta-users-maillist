Dear DeepaMehta users,

as you might have recognised the www.deepamehta.de website is down 
since 3 days. The reason is a severe hardware failure in the computing 
center. It will take a couple of days to setup a new system resp. to 
migrate the server to another computing center. Thank you for your 
patience and understanding.

If you are a DeepaMehta online user, your data/content will most likely 
be restored.

Affected are the DeepaMehta online service, the forum, the newsfeeds, 
the software download, and the interactive demos. Not affected are the 
mail accounts, the mailing lists, the wiki, the blog, and all the 
development tools.

Best regards,
