Dear DeepaMehta friends,

Episode 96 of the Küchenradio podacst contains an interview with 
DeepaMehta co-founder Jörg Richter. In this interview, he reflects 
about computers and cultural criticism: what do we make with machines, 
and what do machines make with us. The interview was recorded on April 
12, 2007 while the re:publica conference in Berlin (in german).

The second half of the podcast contains an interview with re:publica 
co-organistor Markus Beckedahl.
